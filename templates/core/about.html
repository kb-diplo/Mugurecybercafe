Our Story

Serving Kamwangi Since 2020

Mugure Cyber Services was founded with a simple mission: to provide reliable and affordable digital services to the Kamwangi community in Gatundu North.

What started as a small cyber cafe has grown into a comprehensive digital services hub, helping hundreds of customers with their online and technology needs. Our commitment to excellent customer service and quality work has made us a trusted name in the community.
